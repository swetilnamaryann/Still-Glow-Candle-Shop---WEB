About Us

At Still Glow Candles, we're passionate about crafting artisanal candles that evoke warmth, comfort, and serenity. Our small-batch candles are carefully hand-poured with love and attention to detail, using only the finest ingredients and essential oils. From the soft, golden glow of our candles to the subtle, soothing scents that fill the air, every aspect of our candles is designed to transport you to a peaceful, relaxing world. Whether you're seeking to unwind after a long day, create a cozy atmosphere for a special occasion, or simply add a touch of warmth and personality to your home, our candles are the perfect choice.

HOW ITS MADE

"At Still Glow Candles, we take pride in our meticulous candle-making process. Each candle is carefully handcrafted in small batches, ensuring that every detail is meticulously attended to. We begin by selecting the finest, all-natural ingredients, including premium wax, essential oils, and cotton wicks. Our skilled artisans then carefully melt, blend, and pour the wax into elegant glass vessels, creating a truly unique and fragrant work of art. With precision, patience, and a passion for perfection, we pour our heart and soul into every candle, ensuring that it not only illuminates your space but also touches your senses."
